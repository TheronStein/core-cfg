Learn how you can embed other notes and media into your notes. By embedding files in your notes, you can reuse content across your vault.

To embed a file in your vault, add an exclamation mark (`!`) in front of an [Internal link](https://help.obsidian.md/Linking+notes+and+files/Internal+links). You can embed files in any of the [Accepted file formats](https://help.obsidian.md/Files+and+folders/Accepted+file+formats).

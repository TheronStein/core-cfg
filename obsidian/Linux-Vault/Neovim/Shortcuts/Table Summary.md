#### Summary of Common Commands

|Command|Action|
|---|---|
|`h` `j` `k` `l`|Move cursor left, down, up, right|
|`w` `e` `b`|Move by words|
|`dd` `dw` `x`|Delete lines, words, characters|
|`yy` `yw` `p`|Copy, paste|
|`/pattern`|Search|
|`:w` `:q` `:wq`|Save and quit|
|`:e file`|Open file|
|`:split` `:vsplit`|Split windows|
|`q{a-z}` `@{a-z}`|Record & play macros|

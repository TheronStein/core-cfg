## Basic Commands
- **Start new session:** `tmux`
- **List sessions:** `tmux ls`
- **Attach to session:** `tmux attach -t <name>`

## Key Bindings
- **Detach:** `Ctrl+b d`
- **New window:** `Ctrl+b c`
- **Split pane horizontally:** `Ctrl+b "`
- **Split pane vertically:** `Ctrl+b %`
- **Navigate panes:** `Ctrl+b arrow keys`
- **Resize panes:** `Ctrl+b` followed by one of the arrow keys (while holding `Ctrl`)

This reference helps you quickly find important commands and shortcuts to enhance your productivity with tmux.
